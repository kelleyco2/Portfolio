A group project where we created a website for exclusive users to view a library of movies. Our team consisted of three developers: Brendan, Jitender, and myself. The cloud account that holds all the movies is managed by Brendan. The movies will change monthly. I was in charge of the frontend functionality of this site. The biggest challenge was calling to the google drive account and getting a list of all the movies and then immediately making an API call to the movie database to get more information about a specific movie.
